Welcome To LPG

LPG is a fast-growing brand within the premium lubricant oil space, and we intend to innovate the space by leveraging on great opportunities via working within the current market and, together, overcoming industry challenges with a variety of engine oils and to provide more power for every drop of our products.

Who We Are

With a steadfast commitment to excellence and ethical trading practices, we strive to elevate standards and reach new heights in the automotive industry. At LPG, we take pride in our role as exclusive distributors of premium products, ensuring that only the finest quality reaches our valued customers. With four seamlessly integrated divisions, each playing a unique role in our operations, we work tirelessly to exceed expectations and set new benchmarks for success. From importing the highest quality products internationally to serving our local market with unparalleled service, LPG is dedicated to driving innovation and excellence every step of the way. Join us on our journey as we continue to push boundaries and redefine automotive excellence in Bangladesh and beyond.

Our Mission

Our mission is to consistently deliver the highest quality automotive products and lubricants to our customers. We aim to exceed expectations through innovative solutions, ethical practices, and a relentless dedication to customer satisfaction, ensuring that we power progress in every engine.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and leading automotive distributor in Bangladesh and beyond, recognized for our unwavering commitment to excellence, sustainability, and technological advancement in the automotive industry. We envision a future driven by our premium products.
